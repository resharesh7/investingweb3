Privacy policy

We respect your family’s privacy. We handle education records consistent with FERPA and limit access to those with a legitimate educational interest.

Our commitments

  • Access rights: Parents can access and request correction of their child’s education records.
  • Consent: We obtain written consent before sharing personally identifiable information, except where FERPA allows.
  • Security: Encryption in transit, strict role-based access, audit logging, and least-privilege permissions.
  • Training: Staff receive privacy training and follow secure handling practices.

Contact for privacy requests

To request access or an amendment, or to ask a privacy question, contact the school office. We respond promptly and within required timeframes.

Notice

We provide annual notices of rights and updates to our privacy policy. Families are encouraged to review scholarship sites’ privacy policies separately.
